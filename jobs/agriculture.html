Agriculture Workers

Health risks and prevention measures for farm and field work.

About this job

Agriculture involves planting, harvesting, and handling crops or livestock. Hazards include pesticide exposure, tool injuries, animal bites, extreme weather, and repetitive strain. Training and protective measures reduce risks.

Common Diseases and Health Risks

High

Pesticide Poisoning

Skin, eye, and respiratory symptoms due to agrochemicals.

Medium

Tool/Equipment Injuries

Cuts and lacerations from sickles, tractors, and harvesters.

Medium

Animal Bites & Zoonoses

Infections from livestock handling (leptospirosis, brucellosis).

Low

Heat & Weather Exposure

Heat stress, dehydration, and UV damage during field work.
